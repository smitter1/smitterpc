How to Pay for Your Pool

This page explains how to send in your entry fee so we can lock you into the 2025 SmitterPC NCAA Tourney Pool.

The official payment details are shared via email each year so we can keep things semi-private and avoid randoms from the internet sneaking into the pool.

Step 1 — Check Your Email

Look for the annual SmitterPC NCAA invite email in your inbox. It contains:

  • The name of our accountant
  • The current entry fee amount (usually $20)
  • Where to send the funds (bank / app details)
  • The password you'll need for the pool forms

Step 2 — Send Your Payment

Send your payment from a reputable bank or payment service using the instructions in the email. Please:

  • Include your full name in the memo line
  • Use the exact amount listed (no more, no less)
  • Send early enough that it clears before the tournament tips off

Step 3 — Confirm and Play

Once your payment is confirmed, you're officially in. You'll be able to:

  • Submit your bracket using the entry form
  • Track your squad on the Live and Standings pages
  • Claim victory (and cash) if your picks go the distance

If you think your payment might not have gone through, or you haven't received the email with details, reach out to the commish at smitter@smitter.com.
